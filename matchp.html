Preview

Prepare for an intense showdown as the Kolkata Knight Riders face off against the Sunrisers Hyderabad in the highly anticipated IPL 2024 final. Both teams are ready for the ultimate clash. Sunrisers Hyderabad secured their spot after defeating Rajasthan Royals in Qualifier 2, while Kolkata Knight Riders came in strong after a convincing win against Sunrisers Hyderabad in Qualifier 1. Expect a thrilling display of cricketing skill and strategy as these formidable teams battle it out. This promises to be an unforgettable match, keeping fans captivated from the first ball to the last.

In Qualifier 1 of the Indian Premier League, Kolkata Knight Riders emerged victorious against Sunrisers Hyderabad, winning by 8 wickets and securing their place in the IPL 2024 final. Sunrisers Hyderabad were bowled out for 159 in 19.3 overs. Mitchell Starc was impressive with figures of 3/34 in 4 overs, while Varun Chakravarthy and Andre Russell chipped in with 2/26 and 1/15, respectively. In reply, Kolkata chased down the target in just 13.4 overs, finishing at 164/2. Skipper Shreyas Iyer led the charge with a blistering 58 off 24 balls, supported by Venkatesh Iyer’s solid 51 off 28 balls. Rahmanullah Gurbaz contributed 23 runs from 14 balls. For Hyderabad, T Natarajan and Pat Cummins each took a wicket, but their efforts were in vain.

In Qualifier 2 of the Indian Premier League, Sunrisers Hyderabad triumphed over Rajasthan Royals by 36 runs. Batting first, Hyderabad posted a total of 175/9 in 20 overs, with Heinrich Klaasen leading the way with a solid 50 off 34 balls. Rahul Tripathi added a quick 37 off 15 balls, and Travis Head contributed 34 runs from 28 balls. In response, Rajasthan managed only 139/7 in their 20 overs. Shahbaz Ahmed was the standout bowler for Hyderabad, claiming 3 wickets for 23 runs in his 4 overs. Abhishek Sharma supported him well with 2/24 in 4 overs, and T Natarajan was economical, taking 1 wicket for just 13 runs in his 3 overs.
